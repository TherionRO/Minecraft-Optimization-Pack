# Explaining the flavours
## Stable
Choose Optimization Pack stable if you want a stable,reliable,performant and compatible gameplay experience with other mods. Performance may vary depending on your operating system and hardware.

## Rolling Release/Unstable
Choose Optimization Pack Rolling Release/Unstable if you want the newest features and mods that are being verified before landing in the Stable edition. This is only being tested against critical bugs like crashes before releasing, less reliable compared to other editions.

## Vulkan Edition
Choose Optimization Pack Vulkan Edition if you want the absolute best performance and stability,but sacrifice compatibility with other mods as Vulkan renderer has less support overall compared to Sodium. Vulkan Edition is considered the most stable flavour of OP as it comes with the least mods.

## 1.12.2-LTS
Choose Optimization Pack 1.12.2-LTS if you wanna have the best performance and bug fixes in 1.12.2, comes with newer Java versions out of the box using Cleanroom for better support with bleeding-edge hardware. Stability may vary depending on your operating system and hardware
